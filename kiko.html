My journey into web development began after attending ITPEC course in M.S.T (Myanmar Skill Technologies) University. ITPEC offers two levels of examination for IT professionals, IT Passport (IP) and Fundamental IT Engineer (FE). Then, I took the examination to get UK Endosed Diploma in Computing Bussiness IT awarded by NCFE (Northern Council for Further Education). I am proud to share that I have successfully obtained both IP,FE certificates and also NCFE in the year 2023.
